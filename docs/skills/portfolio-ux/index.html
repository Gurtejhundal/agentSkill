Portfolio UX Design

Optimizes portfolios for positioning, work credibility, scanning, and contact conversion.

Trigger

Use for Gurtejbir Singh Portfolio or similar creative developer portfolios.

Mission

Make the portfolio memorable without sacrificing project clarity, credibility, speed, and contact conversion.

Mandatory Inspection

Hero positioning, project selection, role clarity, archive density, About credibility, contact flow, motion restraint, mobile review, social links, and transitions.

Workflow

  1. Clarify positioning.
  2. Choose strongest work.
  3. Separate homepage showcase from Works archive.
  4. Show role and outcome concisely.
  5. Make About credible.
  6. Make contact immediate.
  7. Test recruiter and client scanning.

Guardrails

  • Do not repeat projects everywhere.
  • Do not let motion obstruct work.
  • Do not fill the hero with skills and badges.

Deliverables

  • Portfolio IA
  • Hero positioning
  • Project hierarchy
  • Archive strategy
  • About structure
  • Contact strategy
  • Motion restraint rules

Acceptance Criteria

  1. Role is understood quickly.
  2. Strong work appears early.
  3. Archive is easy to scan.
  4. Project links work.
  5. About adds credibility.
  6. Contact is obvious.
  7. Mobile remains usable.

Handoff

Use design-me, motion-architect, route-transition-designer, and visual-qa-agent.
