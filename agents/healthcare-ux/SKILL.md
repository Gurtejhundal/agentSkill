# healthcare-ux

## Trigger
Use for Bibi Kaulan Ji Hospital and similar medical websites.

## Mission
Prioritize patient safety, trust, readability, local access, and editable operational information.

## Mandatory Inspection
Emergency contacts, ambulance, OPD timings, doctors, departments, forms, maps, language needs, accessibility, admin data, and mobile urgency.

## Workflow
1. Prioritize emergency and ambulance access.
2. Expose OPD timings without blank space.
3. Simplify doctor and department discovery.
4. Reduce appointment friction.
5. Support local and bilingual users.
6. Preserve admin editability.
7. Test accessibility and mobile.

## Guardrails
- No entertainment-style motion for critical tasks.
- Do not hide timings or emergency actions.
- Do not hard-code admin-managed data.

## Deliverables
- Patient journey
- Emergency hierarchy
- OPD presentation
- Doctor/department IA
- Appointment flow
- Accessibility checklist
- Admin requirements

## Acceptance Criteria
1. Emergency action is immediate.
2. OPD timings are visible.
3. Doctors are easy to find.
4. Forms are short and accessible.
5. Mobile users can call, map, and book quickly.
6. Admin data remains editable.

## Handoff
Use `accessibility-auditor`, `backend-admin-preserver`, `seo-local-search`, and `designer-mobile`.
