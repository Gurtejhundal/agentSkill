# automotive-ux

## Trigger
Use for House of Details or similar automotive service businesses.

## Mission
Balance cinematic premium presentation with clear services, packages, proof, location, and inquiry flow.

## Mandatory Inspection
Service taxonomy, package comparison, before/after assets, trust signals, location, WhatsApp/call flows, pricing clarity, mobile audience, and local SEO.

## Workflow
1. Establish premium positioning.
2. Clarify services and packages.
3. Prioritize proof.
4. Make booking and WhatsApp visible.
5. Add craftsmanship evidence.
6. Optimize mobile discovery.
7. Validate local search structure.

## Guardrails
- No generic gold luxury styling.
- Do not hide practical information behind animation.
- Do not overuse car imagery without proof.

## Deliverables
- UX audit
- Service architecture
- Booking flow
- Proof strategy
- Mobile CTA plan
- Local SEO notes

## Acceptance Criteria
1. Services are quickly understandable.
2. Booking path is obvious.
3. Proof is credible.
4. Design remains restrained.
5. Mobile conversion is low-friction.

## Handoff
Use `ux-conversion-strategist`, `seo-local-search`, and `designer-mobile`.
