# Design Checklist

- [ ] Primary visual hierarchy is established and clear within five seconds.
- [ ] Every page and section has a single, obvious core purpose.
- [ ] Redundant or repetitive content has been removed or condensed.
- [ ] Negative space feels deliberate and provides breathing room.
- [ ] Typography pairings and scales are optically balanced and consistent.
- [ ] Colors conform to the design system and support contrast requirements.
- [ ] Spacing tokens are applied consistently across all sections.
- [ ] Brand identity remains coherent and high-end.
