# Healthcare Workflow

Hospital and clinic website design, accessibility, and content hierarchy workflow.

## Sequence
`codebase-recon → healthcare-ux → content-hierarchy → accessibility-auditor → backend-admin-preserver → designer-mobile → interaction-qa-agent → seo-local-search → deployment-verifier`

## Primary Focus
- Quick mobile access to Emergency calling and Ambulance
- Visible OPD timings and department listings
- Assistive technology and keyboard navigation checks
- Protecting editability of operational content in the CMS
- Verification of sitemap, metadata, and local search presence
