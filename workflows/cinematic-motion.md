# Cinematic Motion Workflow

Creates a unified motion system and scroll experiences.

## Sequence
`design-me → motion-architect → scroll-systems-engineer → performance-optimizer → designer-mobile → visual-qa-agent → interaction-qa-agent`

## Primary Focus
- Establish a single, consistent motion language across sections
- Separate time-based and scroll-based motion
- Measure real viewport heights and track scroll triggers
- Maintain performance budget, optimizing WebGL or blur
- Capture start, middle, and end scroll-state screenshots
